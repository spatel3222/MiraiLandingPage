## 5. Unit Economics Deep Dive

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Analysis

**Stated CAC**: ₹45,000

**CAC Calculation Assumptions**:
- Sales team cost: ₹25,000 per client
- Marketing cost: ₹15,000 per client  
- Channel partner commissions: ₹5,000 per client
- **Total CAC**: ₹45,000

**Industry Benchmark**: Legal tech SaaS CAC typically ranges ₹30-60K⁴

### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Calculation

**Stated CLV**: ₹28 lakhs

**CLV Calculation**:
- Average client lifespan: 4 years (assumption based on legal software stickiness)
- Average annual revenue: ₹12 lakhs (weighted average across segments)
- Gross margin: 80% (mature state)
- **CLV Formula**: ₹12,00,000 × 4 years × 0.80 = ₹38.4 lakhs

**Note**: Discrepancy between stated ₹28 lakhs and calculated ₹38.4 lakhs

### LTV/CAC Ratio Analysis

**Stated Ratio**: 6.2x  
**Calculated Ratio**: ₹28,00,000 ÷ ₹45,000 = 6.22x ✓

**Industry Benchmark**: Healthy SaaS LTV/CAC ratio is 3:1 to 5:1⁵

### Net Revenue Retention (NRR)

**Stated NRR**: 125%

**NRR Components**:
- Base retention: 95% (5% annual churn)
- Expansion revenue: 30% from upsells and add-ons
- **Total NRR**: 95% + 30% = 125% ✓

## 6. Break-Even Analysis

### Break-Even Metrics

**Stated Break-Even**: Month 18 at ₹3.2 Crores ARR

**Break-Even Calculation**:
- Fixed costs (annual): ₹2.5 Cr (assumption: team of 25-30 people)
- Variable costs: 22% of revenue (inverse of 78% gross margin)
- Break-even revenue: ₹2.5 Cr ÷ 0.78 = ₹3.2 Cr ✓

**Key Assumptions**:
- Team size: 30 employees by month 18
- Average salary: ₹8.3 lakhs annually
- Infrastructure costs: ₹25 lakhs annually
- Sales & marketing: ₹75 lakhs annually
