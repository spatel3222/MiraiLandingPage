## üìç Stage 1: Client Intake & Initial Contact
**Duration**: 1-7 days  
**Primary Owner**: Intake Team / Marketing  
**Success Metric**: 90%+ qualification accuracy, <24hr response time

### Current State Challenges
- Manual form processing and data entry
- Inconsistent qualification criteria
- Delayed response times (average 48-72 hours)
- Missing conflict checking automation
- Limited intake channel integration

### AI-Enhanced Process Flow

#### 1.1 Lead Generation & Capture
- **AI Chatbots**: 24/7 initial screening on website
- **Smart Forms**: Dynamic questionnaires based on case type
- **Multi-Channel Integration**: Phone, web, email, referral tracking
- **Lead Scoring**: AI-powered qualification algorithms

#### 1.2 Automated Triage
- **Document OCR**: Extract information from uploaded documents
- **Entity Recognition**: Identify parties, dates, locations, claims
- **Case Type Classification**: AI categorization for routing
- **Conflict Checking**: Automated database screening

#### 1.3 Response & Communication
- **Auto-Acknowledgment**: Instant confirmation emails
- **Case Assessment**: Preliminary case strength evaluation
- **Calendar Integration**: Automated consultation scheduling
- **Follow-up Sequences**: Nurture campaigns for prospects

### Technology Stack
- **Intake Software**: Lawmatics, Clio Grow, CloudLex
- **CRM Integration**: HubSpot, Salesforce for legal
- **Document Processing**: AWS Textract, Google Document AI
- **Communication**: Twilio, SendGrid for automated messaging

### KPIs & Metrics
- Response time to initial inquiry: <2 hours
- Qualification accuracy rate: >90%
- Conversion rate (inquiry ‚Üí consultation): >25%
- Client satisfaction with intake process: >4.5/5
