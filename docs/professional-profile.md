## Lead Generation Strategy

### Phase 1: Immediate Actions (Week 1-2)

#### 1. LinkedIn Optimization & Outreach
- **Profile Enhancement**: Add "Book a Call" link in featured section
- **Weekly Publishing**: Transform your "5 AI Workflows" post into a series
- **Direct Outreach**: 
  - Send 10 personalized messages weekly to legal/manufacturing/finance leaders
  - Template: "Noticed you're exploring AI adoption at [Company]. I helped [Similar Company] reduce implementation time by 50%. Worth a 15-minute chat?"
- **Comment Strategy**: Engage on 5 posts daily from target industry leaders

#### 2. HARO (Help a Reporter Out) Activation
- Sign up for HARO immediately
- Respond to 3 relevant queries daily in AI/digital transformation space
- Focus on legal tech, manufacturing automation, fintech categories
- Each response includes your one-liner + link to calendar

#### 3. Free Value Demonstration
- **AI Readiness Assessment**: Create a 10-question assessment for traditional industries
- **Landing Page**: "Is Your [Industry] Ready for AI? Free 5-Minute Assessment"
- **Lead Magnet**: Respondents get personalized AI implementation roadmap
- Share assessment link in all communications

---

### Phase 2: Content Amplification (Week 3-4)

#### 1. "AI Translation" Content Series
Create weekly content that translates Fortune 1 AI for traditional industries:
- **Monday**: "AI Myth vs Reality" posts (debunk common fears)
- **Wednesday**: Case study snippets "How [Traditional Company] Used AI to..."
- **Friday**: "Quick Win" - One AI tool/tactic they can implement today

#### 2. Strategic Guest Posting
Target publications your audience reads:
- **Legal**: Law Technology Today, Legal Tech News
- **Manufacturing**: IndustryWeek, Manufacturing Tomorrow
- **Finance**: The Financial Brand, Bank Director
- Pitch: "5 AI Lessons from Walmart That [Industry] Can Apply Today"

#### 3. Webinar Partnership Strategy
- Partner with industry associations for educational webinars
- Title: "Fortune 1 AI Secrets for [Industry] Leaders"
- Capture leads through registration
- Follow up with consultation offers

---

### Phase 3: Systematic Lead Capture (Month 2)

#### 1. Speaking Bureau Registration
- Submit to: BigSpeak, Executive Speakers Bureau, Leading Authorities
- Create speaker one-sheet with your media profile
- Include: "AI Transformation for Traditional Industries" as signature talk

#### 2. Industry Conference Strategy
- **Legal**: Legaltech, ILTACON
- **Manufacturing**: IMTS, Manufacturing Leadership Summit
- **Finance**: Money20/20, Fintech Meetup
- Apply to speak with topic: "Walmart-Scale AI for [Industry] Innovation"

#### 3. LinkedIn Newsletter Launch
- "The AI Translator" - Weekly insights for non-tech industries
- Include case studies, implementation tips, ROI calculators
- CTA in every edition: "Ready to implement? Let's talk"

#### 4. Strategic Podcast Guesting
- Target industry-specific podcasts (not just tech)
- Pitch: Your startup-to-Fortune 1 journey
- Always mention your consulting availability
