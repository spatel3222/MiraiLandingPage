üó∫Ô∏è Redefined Law Firm Customer Journey: End-to-End Case Management

Total Case Lifecycle: From initial client contact through case closure and retention (Variable: 90 days - 3+ years)

Framework: 8-stage workflow optimized for AI integration and automation opportunities

Stage 1: Client Intake & Initial Contact (1-7 days)

Owner: Intake Team / Marketing | Success Metric: 90%+ qualification accuracy, <24hr response

  • AI Chatbots: 24/7 initial screening on website
  • Document OCR: Extract information from uploaded documents
  • Conflict Checking: Automated database screening
  • Lead Scoring: AI-powered qualification algorithms

Technology Stack: Lawmatics, Clio Grow, AWS Textract, Twilio

Stage 2: Case Triage & Legal Assessment (3-7 days)

Owner: Attorneys / Senior Paralegals | Success Metric: 95%+ accurate evaluation

  • AI Case Law Research: Precedent analysis and outcome prediction
  • Predictive Analytics: Success probability based on similar cases
  • Resource Assessment: Attorney capacity and specialization matching
  • Risk Scoring: Automated risk assessment algorithms

AI Applications: Westlaw Edge, Lexis+ AI, Lex Machina, Premonition

Stage 3: Client Engagement & Agreement (3-10 days)

Owner: Attorneys / Business Development | Success Metric: <5 days to execution

  • Auto-Generated Agreements: Template-based with AI customization
  • E-Signature Integration: DocuSign, HelloSign automation
  • Client Portal Setup: Automated account creation and access
  • Payment Processing: Instant retainer processing and confirmation

Technology Integration: DocuSign, LawPay, MyCase, NetDocuments

Stage 4: Matter Setup & Discovery Planning (7-14 days)

Owner: Case Management / Paralegals | Success Metric: Complete setup in <10 days

  • Matter Management: Automated folder structure and organization
  • AI-Optimized Allocation: Resource assignment based on case complexity
  • Discovery Strategy: AI-assisted strategy development
  • Timeline Automation: Automated scheduling based on court rules

AI Capabilities: Case prediction, outcome forecasting, risk assessment

Stage 5: Evidence Collection & Document Discovery (30-180 days)

Owner: Discovery Team / Litigation Support | Success Metric: 95%+ completeness, <5% privilege violations

  • AI-Assisted Review: Technology-assisted review (TAR) implementation
  • Privilege Detection: Automated attorney-client privilege screening
  • Smart Deduplication: Intelligent duplicate detection and removal
  • Relevance Scoring: ML-powered document ranking

Technology Stack: Relativity, Everlaw, Kira Systems, DISCO

Stage 6: Legal Work & Case Development (60-365+ days)

Owner: Attorneys / Legal Team | Success Metric: Milestone completion on schedule

  • Automated Research: AI-powered case law and statute analysis
  • Brief Generation: AI-assisted legal memoranda and motion drafting
  • Scenario Modeling: AI-powered case outcome simulations
  • Strategic Recommendations: AI insights for case strategy

Advanced AI: Legal Writing AI, Expert Witness Matching, Jury Analytics

Stage 7: Resolution & Settlement (14-90 days)

Owner: Lead Attorney / Settlement Team | Success Metric: Optimal outcome, >4.8/5 satisfaction

  • Settlement Intelligence: AI-powered settlement value calculation
  • Negotiation Analytics: Data-driven negotiation recommendations
  • Risk vs. Reward: Probability analysis of trial vs. settlement
  • Trial Preparation: AI-assisted witness and exhibit organization

Technology Integration: Settlement platforms, trial presentation, jury research

Stage 8: Case Closure & Client Retention (7-30 days)

Owner: Case Management / Client Relations | Success Metric: 100% file completion, 90%+ retention

  • Automated Archival: Document organization and indexing
  • AI-Generated Reports: Case completion and outcome summaries
  • Satisfaction Tracking: Automated feedback collection and analysis
  • Relationship Management: Future legal needs identification and referral programs

Long-term Value: Client intelligence, cross-selling automation, loyalty programs
