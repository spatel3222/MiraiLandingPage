Risk Assessment & Mitigation

Comprehensive risk management framework with proven mitigation strategies

HIGH RISK

LLM Performance Inconsistency

AI responses may be unpredictable or inaccurate, affecting customer trust

Mitigation: Extensive testing protocols, fallback mechanisms, human escalation paths, real-time quality monitoring
HIGH RISK

Integration Complexity

Complex integration with existing Walmart systems may cause delays or failures

Mitigation: Phased rollout approach, extensive API testing, rollback procedures, alternative integration paths
MEDIUM RISK

Customer Adoption Rate

Customers may resist AI-powered shopping experiences or find them confusing

Mitigation: Intuitive UX design, comprehensive support, value demonstration, feedback integration
MEDIUM RISK

Competitive Response

Amazon or other competitors may accelerate their AI initiatives in response

Mitigation: Leverage physical store advantage, exclusive partnerships, continuous innovation, rapid iteration
LOW RISK

Regulatory Changes

New AI regulations may impact implementation or require additional compliance

Mitigation: Privacy-first design, ethical AI principles, regulatory monitoring, compliance frameworks
LOW RISK

Technology Evolution

Rapid AI advancement may make current approaches obsolete

Mitigation: Modular architecture, continuous learning systems, technology partnerships, adaptation protocols
